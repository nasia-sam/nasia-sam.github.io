Fosdem 2019 & 2020

February 02-03/2019 & 01-02/2020
Brussels, Belgium
Fosdem is the biggest opensource conference that is held every year the first weekend of February in Brussels. I once suggested at our IEEE SB meeting the idea of attending the conference and some people were intested so we booked a flight and went to Brussels. The conference is (and i can't strees that enough) A M A Z I N G ! We met people from all over the world, we went at talks that were way advanced for us, we worked as volunteers, we drunk belgium beers, we ate sooo many waffles, walked in the city and I helped my friends with SQL cause it was exams period. Best thing about Fosdem? You can talk to anyone and anyone will talk to you. We met people who worked at Firefox, Postgres, VLC etc. We got drunk while drinking beers with some folks from Google. I loved every single moment from this trip, both years. Get some friends and go to Fosdem next year.
